How it Works

Features of TickTock

TickTock is a time tracker designed to help businesses monitor and manage the time their employees spend on tasks, projects, and work hours. Users can track their work hours, breaks, and attendance electronically, while managers can oversee employee productivity, monitor overtime, and generate reports for payroll and project management purposes.

Features of TickTock include:

  • Clock in/clock out functionality: Users can clock in and out to log their time spent working.
  • Report generation: Users can generate reports about their working hours and time spent on specific projects.
  • Task and project Tracking: Users can attribute working hours to projects they have been assigned.
  • Different user permissions: Users have access to different features based on whether they are employees, managers, or administrators.
